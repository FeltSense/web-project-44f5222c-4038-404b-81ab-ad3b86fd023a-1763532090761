# RetailHub - Modern Retail Discovery Platform

## Project Overview
RetailHub is a modern retail business website designed for general consumers. The platform combines social discovery, product ratings, trending content, and community engagement to create an exceptional shopping experience.

## Design Philosophy
- **Modern & Professional**: Clean, contemporary design with a focus on usability
- **User-Centric**: Intuitive navigation and clear calls-to-action
- **Social-First**: Community-driven features for discovery and sharing
- **Trust-Building**: Verified reviews, testimonials, and transparent pricing

## Key Features

### 1. Rating & Voting System
- 5-star product rating system
- Community voting on helpful reviews
- Verified purchase badges for authenticity
- Detailed review system with upvoting

### 2. Social Discovery
- Follow shoppers with similar tastes
- Personalized recommendations
- Community discussions and Q&A
- Share finds across social platforms

### 3. Trending Content
- Real-time trending products dashboard
- Algorithm-based popularity tracking
- Category-specific trending items
- Community engagement metrics

### 4. Pricing
- Simple, transparent $29/month subscription
- All-inclusive feature access
- 30-day money-back guarantee
- Secure Stripe payment integration

### 5. Testimonials
- Authentic customer reviews
- Professional photos and detailed feedback
- Verified user badges
- Helpful vote counts

### 6. Team Profiles
- Contact information prominently displayed
- Multiple communication channels
- 24/7 support for premium members
- Social media integration

## Technical Stack
- **Framework**: Next.js 14 with TypeScript
- **Styling**: Tailwind CSS
- **Icons**: Lucide React
- **Forms**: Custom form handling with API integration
- **Payment**: Stripe checkout integration

## Color Scheme
- Primary: Blue (#2563eb) - Trust and professionalism
- Secondary: Purple (#9333ea) - Creativity and innovation
- Accent: Yellow (#fbbf24) - Highlights and CTAs
- Neutrals: Gray scale for balance

## Target Audience
- General consumers seeking better shopping experiences
- Social shoppers who value community recommendations
- Product enthusiasts who enjoy sharing discoveries
- Value-conscious buyers looking for authentic reviews

## Conversion Goals
1. Sign-ups for premium membership
2. Community engagement (reviews, ratings, discussions)
3. Social sharing and referrals
4. Contact form submissions for inquiries
